The Triple-Thread Algorithm
Three pillars.
One pattern.
The generation logic is built on three distinct pillars from the Dream-to-Pattern classification. Each pillar drives a separate visual variable.
01
Pillar I — Structure
Figurative Analysis

Extracts the "what" of the dream. Drives line quality and density. High-detail dreams produce intricate motifs; abstract dreams produce fluid, broad shapes.

02
Pillar II — Palette
Emotional Impact

Analyses the "feeling" — fear, joy, confusion. Drives the colour palette. Nightmare maps to dark/red. Heavenly maps to soft/pastel/smooth.

EMOTIONAL CHARGE—
03
Pillar III — Composition
Environmental Architecture

Analyses the "where." Dictates layout and symmetry. Expansive landscapes produce radial open compositions; claustrophobic spaces produce dense geometries.
